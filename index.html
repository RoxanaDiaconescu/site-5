Corporate Overview

BOC AGRO SPEED S.R.L. is a leading enterprise operating within the Wholesale Trade sector in Romania. Headquartered in Falticeni, Suceava, our company acts as a vital conduit connecting local farming cooperatives with international manufacturing and retail markets.

We specialize in the procurement and wholesale distribution of farm product raw materials, with a primary focus on high-yield grain and field beans. True to our name, "Agro Speed", we prioritize rapid, secure logistics and supply chain efficiency, ensuring that agricultural commodities reach their destination in optimal condition.

Agro Speed Connect App

To modernize agricultural trade and streamline logistics for our partners, BOC AGRO SPEED S.R.L. develops and maintains a proprietary B2B mobile application published on the Google Play Store.

The Agro Speed Connect app empowers farmers, grain elevators, and wholesale buyers to manage their supply chain digitally. Users can track shipments via GPS, monitor live commodity prices, and execute wholesale contracts directly from their Android devices.

  • Real-time market tracking for grain and field beans.
  • Digital contract generation for B2B wholesale trade.
  • Live GPS logistics tracking for agricultural freight.
  • Instant push notifications for delivery and inventory status.
